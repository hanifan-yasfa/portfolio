ChatBar AI is a real-time, voice-enabled AI chat that learns directly from your website (or any internal docs) and answers visitors 24/7. It’s built to match your brand’s look and tone, so the conversation feels native to your site.
I led the development of ChatBar AI’s brand guidelines, focusing on crafting a tone that reflects a professional and understated visual identity. The design approach prioritizes clarity and restraint, ensuring the brand feels polished without being visually overwhelming.
Alongside this, I worked as a video editor, creating simple motion graphics to enhance content delivery and maintain consistency across visual touchpoints.
